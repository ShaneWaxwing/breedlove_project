More about me

I started this business in 2019 after working in the grooming environment for over 4 years. Although grooming is still a passion of mine, I found the fast paced style was detached and made it harder to form a close bond with the dogs. Because of that I have branched out to other services that go hand in hand with at home grooming. Not only do I groom your dog in your home where they are most comfortable, I also provide pet sitting, dog walking and house cleaning. I wanted to provide a more personal experience for both the pet and the parent. The services I provide go above and beyond what you normally get when going to the groomer.
